About Colby

I was born in Johnston, IA, a Des Moines suburb, on October 21st, 2004. I grew up with an older brother and a younger sister, and my parents both worked at the same location in downtown Des Moines at Principal Financial Group. Currently, I am a 21 year old junior at the University of Iowa pursuing a double major in Business Analytics and Information Systems and Finance, as well as a certificate in Entreprenuerial Management.

Some hobbies that I enjoy are hanging out with friends, catching up on TV shows and movies, and playing various sports (namely basketball, soccer, and golf). I live in an apartment complex with three other roommates who are my closest friends. I tend to spend my weekends relaxing in Iowa City, and I enjoy visiting back home to see family and hometown friends over school breaks. This summer I plan on getting an internship at a business that is close to home so I can spend more time with family and be close to those that I cherish.
